MCL Cooking System (mCS)

The mCS, or the MCL Cooking System, operates on mPellets, an eco-friendly fuel derived from organic waste and biomass, to replace LPG expertly produced by our dedicated network of local farmers. This innovative cooking solution not only transforms traditional cooking practices but also fosters a sustainable ecosystem, empowering farmers to contribute to renewable energy production. By utilizing organic waste and biomass, the mCS exemplifies a circular economy, minimizing environmental impact and maximizing the use of locally sourced resources for a cleaner, greener future.

mCoal

Revolutionizing the energy sector, MCL's mCoal provides a sustainable and eco-friendly alternative to fossil coal, empowering millions of farmers to become energy producers and combating global warming.

CARBON NEGATIVE BIOFUELS

To replace fossil fuels completely for all applications and in existing infrastructure, biomass is grown as raw material for biofuels. Not only does biomass absorb the emissions from biofuel burning, but it also sequesters the existing carbon in the atmosphere into the soil

ORGANIC FARMING & CHEMICAL FREE FOOD

To replace chemical (cancerous) farming completely with organic farming and produce various processed foods using it

MCL SUSTAINABLE PRODUCTS (MSP)

To replace all petrochemical products with sustainable products (produced from various organic raw materials) that will cater to our every need while also taking care of our Mother Nature and the environment.
