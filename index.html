Our Projects :- Mcoal, MCL Bio-CNG Project, MCL Village Project (MVP).
Mcoal :- mCoal offers a sustainable solution to replace fossil coal with carbon-negative bio coal, benefiting both farmers and the environment. Through energy farming with napier grass, rural communities can reduce their carbon footprint and improve their economic impact while promoting soil fertility!
MVP Project :- MCL's Village Project (MVP) is a comprehensive effort to promote the development of farmers and rural entrepreneurs in India, with a focus on adopting sustainable and organic farming practices. By centering our business model around the Gram Panchayat and providing a one-stop solution, we aim to improve the standard of living and income of rural communities while phasing out chemical farming methods and promoting reusable products !
